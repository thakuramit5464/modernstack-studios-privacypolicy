Privacy Policy

ModernStack Calc

Effective Date: June 28, 2026

Introduction

This Privacy Policy applies to the ModernStack Calc application (hereby referred to as "the App") for mobile devices, created by Amit Kumar Singh ("the Developer") as a free service. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

ModernStack Calc is a modern calculator application designed to perform standard arithmetic calculations. The App operates completely offline and does not require users to create an account or log in.

Data Collection

The Developer respects your privacy. The App does not directly collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information.

Specifically, the App does not collect or access:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Contacts
  • Photos or videos
  • Files
  • Messages
  • Location (precise or approximate)
  • Camera access
  • Microphone access
  • Calendar
  • Clipboard contents
  • Biometric information
  • Payment information

Local Storage

To provide a seamless user experience, the App stores certain information only locally on your device. This includes:

  • Calculation history
  • Theme preference (Light, Dark, or System)
  • Calculator settings
  • User preferences stored using Android DataStore

This information never leaves your device. You can completely remove this data at any time by clearing the App's data in your device settings or by uninstalling the App.

Permissions

ModernStack Calc is designed to respect your device's security and requests only the absolute minimum permissions necessary for its core functionality.

The App does not request any dangerous Android runtime permissions. It operates fully within its sandboxed environment without requiring intrusive access to your device's resources.

Third-Party Services

ModernStack Calc operates independently.

ModernStack Calc does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or crash-reporting services. (e.g., no Firebase Analytics, Firebase Crashlytics, Google AdMob, etc.).

The App relies only on standard Android frameworks and Google Play Services required for general app distribution via the Google Play Store.

Data Sharing

Because the App does not collect any personal data, there is no data to share. Specifically:

  • No personal data is sold.
  • No personal data is rented.
  • No personal data is shared with third parties.
  • No user information is transmitted to external servers by the App.

Children's Privacy

ModernStack Calc is suitable for general audiences. The Developer does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided personal information, please contact the Developer, though as stated, the App fundamentally does not collect personal data.

Data Security

The security of your experience is a priority. Because all App data (such as calculation history and preferences) remains exclusively on your local device and is never transmitted over the internet by the App, the risk of data interception or breach during transmission is virtually eliminated.

However, please be aware that no method of electronic storage on a mobile device is 100% secure, and the Developer cannot guarantee absolute security of the device itself.

User Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (such as under GDPR or CCPA), you may have rights regarding your personal data (access, modification, deletion).

Because ModernStack Calc does not collect, hold, or process any personal information, there is generally no personal data held by the Developer for you to access, modify, or delete. You have complete control over your local data and can delete it by uninstalling the App or clearing its data on your device.

Policy Updates
